What's the point?

I wanted to see the correlations between Bitcoin and other assets, this is some what of a research paper. Your more than welcome to look at the graphs rather than reading, but do note that just because there is a correlation showing does NOT mean it to be true. There are outliers that can effect the accuracy of correlations but for the sake of easily understanding, and not reading deeply on google about what's going on, we won't worry about outliers.

We use correlation coefficients to identify price behaviour in the past. It also helps us with Risk management, by choosing uncorrelated assets allowing us to have a diversified portfolio. We can use it for balancing our portfolio by reallocating funds to insure we are diversified.

"Concentration to get rich, Diversification to stay wealthy"
